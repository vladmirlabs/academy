These investors are happy with the success of Vlad'mir Labs and they wish to support the Vlad'mir Labs in launching its investment program. This will allow the Vlad'mir Labs team to invest in other crypto projects using the funds allocated by private investors to the Vlad'mir Labs team.

After a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) and partnership deal was signed, Private investors receive their invested funds in the long term through a strategic exit plan for their investment, while the Vlad'mir Labs team also earns returns. Additionally, the earnings that the Vlad'mir Labs team receives will be used for the expansion and development of the Vlad'mir Labs project.
